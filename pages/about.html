About

Homefinder, created by Anton Kireyev

Homefinder is designed to provide users with a dynamic and interactive platform to explore the United States and find their next home! Currently only available with static U.S Census data for MSA regions

Created with the use of:

  • Bootstrap
  • LeafletJS
  • FastAPI
  • United States Census
    • Population and Housing Data: Decennial Census (2020)
    • Labor, Commuting, and Income Information: ACS
    • Reference Files (Location data): Published by the CBP
